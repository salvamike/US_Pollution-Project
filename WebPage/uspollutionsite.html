Introduction

This final project it is Based on a decade of air quality data from 2012-2022, can we determine the top 5 most overall polluted cilties and top 5 overall least polluted cities and can we use these findings to predict the 2023 AQI levels?


Research Questions
1. What are the top 5 most overall polluted cities based on AQI data?
2. What are the top 5 most overall least polluted cities based on AQI data?
3. Can we predict the next year of pollution levels for these cities?
